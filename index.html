Who We Are

From Selling Toys to Building Dreams

In 2024, we transitioned from CreativeKids, a successful toy company, into Creative Kids. We realized our true passion wasn't selling products, but empowering the hands that use them.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To improve and nurture creativity in children through accessible programs, hands-on workshops, and free resources.

Vision: A future where every child sees themselves as a confident, capable, and lifelong creative problem-solver.

Our Values

  • Inspiration
  • Inclusion
  • Innovation
  • Impact

Our Programs

Sparking imagination through hands-on experiences.

In-Person

Creativity Workshops

High-energy, hands-on sessions focused on design thinking, visual arts, storytelling, and imaginative play. Our workshops are free or offered on a sliding scale for qualifying families.

Education

School Partnerships

Collaborative programs that integrate creativity into existing school curricula (S.T.E.A.M., Language Arts, Social Studies). We provide training and materials.

Outreach

Innovation Labs & Events

Dedicated spaces equipped with tools for electronics and crafts, plus large public events like "Maker Fairs" designed to bring families together.
